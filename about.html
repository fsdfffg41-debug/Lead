About MapLeads

We're building the easiest way for agencies and businesses to find offline companies ready for digital services.

Our Mission

To unlock the untapped market of 50M+ businesses without a web presence. We make lead generation simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone—from solo freelancers to enterprise agencies.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a team of data engineers and growth hackers frustrated with expensive lead gen tools, MapLeads started with a simple question: "Why is scraping Google Maps data so complicated?"

We built MapLeads to answer that. Today, 12,000+ developers use our API to find leads across 30+ countries. Our customers have closed millions in deals targeting offline businesses.

Frequently asked questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes — change your plan anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Upgrades are prorated immediately.
Is there a free trial for Pro and Agency?
Pro includes a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. Agency plans include a custom trial period — contact sales to discuss.
What happens when I exceed my monthly limits?
We'll notify you by email when you reach 80% of your limit. You can upgrade anytime, or purchase additional credits as a top-up without changing your plan.
Do you offer annual billing?
Yes. Switch to annual billing in your dashboard to save 20% — that's over 2 months free. Toggle the switch above to see annual pricing.
Do I need a Google Maps API key?
No — that's the whole point of MapLeads. We handle all the infrastructure. You just make requests to our API and get clean data back. No Google credentials required.
Is the data GDPR-compliant?
MapLeads only indexes publicly listed business data (not personal data). We recommend users comply with local regulations for their outreach campaigns. See our Privacy Policy for details.
